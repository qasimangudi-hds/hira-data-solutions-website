How it works

Your data, unified — and answerable

We bring your scattered data together into one reliable place, then put a conversational interface on top. Your team asks questions the way they'd ask a colleague.

Connects to your existing tools

Salesforce, Google Sheets, Xero, HubSpot and more. We do the integration — you don't change how you work.

One place for all your data

Everything consolidated into a single, reliable source — with the definitions we agreed in the audit. No more hunting across three spreadsheets for one number.

Just ask the question

Available on Telegram, Teams or Slack. Type a question in plain English and get a clear answer — no SQL, no dashboards, no training needed.

Built for busy teams

Designed for founders and ops managers who need answers fast, not for analysts. If you can send a message, you can use it.
