Bespoke AI builds and workflows.

Off-the-shelf AI tools rarely fit how your business actually runs. We design and build custom AI solutions — automations, agents and workflows — around the specific use cases the audit surfaces, so the technology fits your work, not the other way round.

What it is

AI built around your business

Once the audit has pinpointed where AI can earn its keep, we build it. That might be automating a manual process, putting an AI agent on a repetitive task, or wiring AI into the tools your team already uses. Everything sits on the data foundations the audit establishes — so what we build is reliable, not a clever demo that falls over in real use.

What we build

From automations to agents

Different problems need different builds. We scope each one to the specific use case, rather than reaching for a one-size-fits-all tool.

Workflow automation

Automate the repetitive, manual work that quietly eats your team's time — data entry, reporting, routing, reminders and follow-ups.

Custom AI agents

Purpose-built assistants and agents that handle a specific job end to end, grounded in your own data, rules and ways of working.

AI-powered tools & integrations

AI wired into the systems you already use, so it shows up where your team works — not as yet another tab to remember to open.

How it works

From use case to live

A focused build process with a clear shape — scoped tightly so you know what you're getting and when.

  1. Scope the use case

    We start from a concrete use case the audit surfaced, and agree exactly what success looks like before we build anything.

  2. Design

    We design the build — what it does, where it fits in your workflow, and how your team will actually use it day to day.

  3. Build & test

    We build it and test it against real cases, refining until it's dependable on your actual data — not just on a happy-path demo.

  4. Deploy & iterate

    We put it live, support your team through adoption, and keep improving it as you use it and your needs change.
