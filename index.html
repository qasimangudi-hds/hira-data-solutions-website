Who We Are

Built by people who've done the work

Hira Data Solutions was founded in 2023 by Qasim Angudi and Haider Variava — two mathematicians who met at university and spent the next decade building data functions at some of Europe's most demanding organisations.

We started Hira because we saw a gap: growing businesses needed the kind of senior analytics thinking that large enterprises take for granted — but couldn't justify a full-time hire. We offer that expertise on your terms, at a scale that works for you.

Together with our team of handpicked specialists, we’ve delivered projects across a wide range of industries and continue to build out our capabilities and services globally.

The name Hira is inspired by the Cave of Hira — the place where the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) would retreat to reflect and seek clarity. It holds deep significance as the birthplace of revelation in Islam.
